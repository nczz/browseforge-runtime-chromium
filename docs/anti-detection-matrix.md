Status vocabulary: `pass` means committed evidence exists; `warn` means accepted alpha risk with evidence; `fail` means evidence shows a defect; `not_tested` means no detector evidence exists; `not_applicable` means the surface does not apply; `blocked` means required artifact/evidence is missing; `manual` means the artifact exists but validation requires the target OS or operator step.

macOS x64 is intentionally `blocked` until a real `browseforge-runtime-chromium-v0.1.0-alpha.0-macos-x64.zip` is produced from the source checkout and accompanied by SHA-256, SBOM, and provenance. After packaging, it remains `not_tested` for launch and detector evidence unless separate evidence is produced. Do not create placeholder archives or reuse another platform artifact.
